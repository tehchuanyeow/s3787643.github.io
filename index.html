My Project Idea

The Overview

I had an idea for a third person shooter styled linear-based game with choices that shape and influence the gameplay, as well as a "Personality" meter. The gameplay will be similar to that of most triple-A title games with moving and shooting capabilities (Ex: The Last of Us, Grand Theft Auto, etc.), choices will appear according to scripted events and each choice made by the player will make their character turn more evil or good from this "Personality" meter, but also affect the characters around the player, meaning that There is always a catch to who you emphasise more and what the player makes of to be more of a priority.

Motivation

My motivation behind the idea of this game is derived from story-based games, such as Telltale's Walking Dead, or Dontnod's Life is Strange. But the choices never really seemed to actually matter. Meaning like you could save a character from dying in one episode, only for them to die in another way in the next episode due to problems with continuity, or that they aren't major characters anymore to save the developers time from the workload of having to constantly put in alternate scenarios for alternate choices. I feel like with my game, I could do just that, and more.

Game Description

The story and gameplay will influence the whole game and not just choices, but the character's "Personality", which could lead to very different alternate endings with at least multiple outcomes with multiple characters of which can be determinant to save. My focus will be more on the either or situation. Meaning you either save one character or the other and major events would occur based on such a choice, while also affecting your character's Personality. The different outcomes & gameplay are based entirely on the types of characters that you choose to help and how high/low your Personality meter is. There is no such thing as being a directly good/bad player in this game, meaning that it is more about judge of characters and their ulterior motives, sort of like a psychological game. The third person shooting mechanic will deeply involve stealth mechanics and not every scenario presented will necessarily have shooting in it. But there is no game over for failing certain obstacles, meaning that if something is missed or failed to get through, it will simply play as an effect on the character's personality both positively and negatively.

The Gameplay

Gameplay will focus on "Personality Meter" & Choices in a linear-based storyline that although are just factors, play a major role on the influence of the gameplay and direction of the Story of the Game, as well as the scenarios that differ. The view for the player is Third-Person. However, some of the scenarios involved do not always involve weapon mechanics, but relies more on stealth. There is also optional customisability to change the looks of the character. The different scenarios involved will also contain different choices for players to make for their character, such as influencing relationships in the game or a certain set of skills for other purposes, etc. And the Personality Meter will determine the final ending, together with a few foreshadowing moments that display the current emphasis on how good/bad the character currently is.

Scenarios

The different scenarios that can occur but are not limited to are:

Scenario Effects
Relationships Major decision that directly influences relationship and "Personality" Meter in some circumstances
"Good" Choices Might have a Positive impact on the Player, but might also negatively affect other factors
"Bad" Choices Might have a Negative impact on the Player, but could also assist other factors
Personality Meter Influences what scenarios that will take place in the future if the player chooses to continue their current route

The Storyline

It is still a rough concept, but the storyline will take place throughout 20 in-game weeks that will have a playable and customisable Main Character, followed by different side characters who will make their first appearances that take place during the events of the game. Their presence and relationships with the player throughtout the rest of the game from there on will be determined by the choices that the player themselves will make to choose between their character and the relationship choices (Also known as 'Determinant Characters' in common gaming terms). The main character will be present throughout all situations and they will not be able to be killed off until the ending if the player gets certain endings. The storyline will also consist of shooting, stealth, fighting and other events that can also be determined if the player chooses to carry out these certain scenarios. There will be a tutorial in the beginning that will cover all of these events for the player for ease of gameplay in future events.

Ultimately this will raise the bar for future games (especially for those in story-telling) if it succeeds.
